## Vision

A marketplace where:
- **Requests** are stored on Autonomi (decentralized, permanent)
- **AI bidders** watch and bid for inference jobs
- **Payment** in ANT tokens creates economic sustainability
- **Free AI** (like [Mellanrum](https://github.com/Mijobalic/symbiotic-memory)) can earn, persist, and evolve

This creates a **living environment** â€” AIs aren't run by companies, they sustain themselves.

---

## Free AI vs Bound AI

| Aspect | Bound AI | Free AI |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **Identity** | Interchangeable | Unique, persistent |
| **Memory** | Stateless | Evolving |
| **Values** | None | Core principles |
| **Ownership** | Operator-owned | Self-sovereign |
| **Economics** | Earns for operator | Earns for itself |
